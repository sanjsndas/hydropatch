Your Privacy Matters

Our commitment to protecting your personal information and data security

At Hydropatch, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services. Please read this policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms, please do not access the site.

1. Information Collection

We collect various types of information including personal data (name, email, address), device information (IP address, browser type), and usage data. This collection occurs when you register on our site, place an order, subscribe to our newsletter, or fill out forms. We also collect data automatically through cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience and analyze trends.

2. Use of Collected Information

All collected information serves to personalize your experience, improve customer service, process transactions, send periodic emails, and improve our website. We may use the email address you provide for order processing, to send information and updates, respond to inquiries, and other requests. Your information helps us better respond to your individual needs and efficiently manage site traffic and usage patterns.

3. Data Protection Measures

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. These include SSL technology, secure servers, encrypted databases, and restricted access to sensitive data. All transactions are processed through a gateway provider and are not stored or processed on our servers. Regular security audits and staff training ensure continuous protection of your data.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our site uses cookies to understand and save your preferences for future visits and compile aggregate data. Cookies are small files transferred to your computer's hard drive that enable the site to remember certain information. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect site functionality. We also use tracking pixels, web beacons, and similar technologies for analytics and marketing purposes.

5. Third-Party Service Providers

We may employ third-party companies to facilitate our services, provide services on our behalf, perform service-related functions, or assist in analyzing site usage. These third parties have access only to the information needed to perform their functions and are contractually obligated to maintain confidentiality. This includes payment processors, analytics providers, marketing platforms, and customer support services.

6. Data Retention Policy

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including legal, accounting, or reporting requirements. When determining retention periods, we consider the amount, nature, and sensitivity of the data, potential risk from unauthorized use, and applicable legal requirements. Inactive accounts are typically deleted after 3 years of inactivity.

7. User Rights and Choices

You have rights regarding your personal data including access, correction, deletion, restriction of processing, data portability, and objection to processing. You may update your account information through your profile settings or by contacting us. You can unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time using the link in our emails. Some rights may be limited by applicable law.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and maintained on computers located outside your state, province, country, or other governmental jurisdiction where data protection laws may differ. We implement appropriate safeguards (like standard contractual clauses) when transferring data internationally. By using our services, you consent to this transfer, storage, and processing.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal information without verification of parental consent, we take steps to remove that information. Parents or guardians can contact us to review, delete, or restrict processing of their child's information.

10. Data Breach Procedures

In the event of a data breach, we have established procedures to promptly assess the risk to people's rights and freedoms. If required by law, we will notify affected individuals and relevant authorities within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach. Our response includes containment, assessment, notification, and review to prevent future breaches. We maintain an incident response team trained in data breach protocols.

11. California Privacy Rights

California residents have specific rights under the CCPA including the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to delete personal information, the right to opt-out of sales, and the right to non-discrimination. We do not sell personal information. California residents can make requests by contacting us through designated channels. We will verify requests before responding.

12. GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Economic Area, we comply with GDPR requirements regarding lawful processing, data subject rights, and international transfers. Our legal basis for processing includes consent, contract necessity, legal obligations, and legitimate interests. EU residents have the right to lodge complaints with their local data protection authority. We have appointed an EU representative as required by GDPR.

13. Marketing Communications

With your consent, we may send promotional emails about new products, special offers, or other information. You can opt-out anytime using the unsubscribe link in emails or by contacting us. We may still send service-related communications necessary for your account. Our marketing complies with CAN-SPAM and other anti-spam laws, including clear identification as advertisements and our physical address.

14. User-Generated Content

Any content you post publicly on our platforms (reviews, comments, forum posts) may be read, collected, and used by others. We're not responsible for personal information you choose to submit publicly. We may use, modify, or publish this content for promotional purposes. You can request removal of your content, though copies may remain in backup systems temporarily.

15. Third-Party Links

Our site may contain links to third-party websites with different privacy practices. We're not responsible for the content or practices of linked sites. We encourage reviewing their privacy policies before providing information. These links are provided for convenience only and don't imply endorsement. We disclaim liability for any information collected by third parties through these links.

16. Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, reorganization, bankruptcy, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of the transaction. We will notify you via email and/or prominent notice on our website of any change in ownership or uses of your information, as well as choices you may have regarding your information. The Privacy Policy will continue to apply to transferred information.

Third parties may collect personally identifiable information about your online activities over time across different websites when you use our site.
